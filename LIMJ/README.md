# LinkedIn Master Journalist (LIMJ) Skill

**Autonomous generation of LinkedIn articles, dual social posts, and branded cover images.**

## What This Skill Does

LIMJ transforms a high-level brief into three production-ready files:

1. **Article.md** — 1,200–1,800 word LinkedIn article optimized for C-suite readers
2. **Posts.md** — Two social post variants (personal + company page) with hashtag research and engagement strategy
3. **Cover.html** — Self-contained 1200×627px LinkedIn OpenGraph cover with PNG download button

## How It Works

1. **Clarification**: LIMJ asks for missing context (audience, tone, data, brand)
2. **Research**: Web search for stats, competitive landscape, hashtags, engagement targets
3. **Generation**: Drafts all three files following strict voice, structure, and quality rules
4. **Validation**: Runs quality gates on vocabulary, credibility, design specs
5. **Output**: All three files ready to publish

## Key Features

- **Autonomous**: Asks for missing info, never assumes
- **Credible**: Every statistic sourced, every claim defensible
- **Voice**: Conversational authority, not corporate jargon
- **Designed**: Professional LinkedIn-optimized cover images
- **Replicable**: Exact methodology for others to follow
