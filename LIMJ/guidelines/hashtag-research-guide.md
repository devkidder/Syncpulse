# Hashtag Research Guide

## Volume Tiers

### High Volume (1M+ followers)

Use 1-2 for **broad discoverability**:
- #AI
- #Leadership
- #Startups
- #Technology
- #Innovation

**Risk**: Lost in noise; your post will be buried quickly

**Reward**: Massive reach if content hits algorithm

### Medium Volume (100K-1M followers)

Use 2-3 for **targeted reach**:
- #LegalTech
- #BlockchainSecurity
- #SaaS
- #FounderLife
- #StartupStory

**Best tier** for most LIMJ posts. Balance reach + specificity.

### Niche Volume (<100K followers)

Use 1-2 for **community specificity**:
- #ArbitrationLaw
- #Web3Gaming
- #FederalHousing
- #DarknetResearch

**Reward**: Engaged community of experts

**Risk**: Limited reach, but highly targeted

## Research Methodology

1. **Search the topic on LinkedIn**: "LinkedIn hashtag AI legal research"
2. **Note hashtags appearing in competitor posts**
3. **Check hashtag follower counts**: Click the hashtag on LinkedIn
4. **Look for emerging hashtags**: New but growing (#TAG that got 50K new followers this month)
5. **Cross-reference on Twitter/X**: See if hashtags are trending there too

## Recommended Mix for LIMJ Posts

**Total hashtags**: 5-7

**Distribution**:
- High volume: 1-2
- Medium volume: 2-3
- Niche: 1-2

**Example for legal tech article**:
- #AI (high)
- #LegalTech (medium)
- #StartupLife (medium)
- #SmallBusiness (medium)
- #ArbitrationLaw (niche)
- #LegalInnovation (niche)
