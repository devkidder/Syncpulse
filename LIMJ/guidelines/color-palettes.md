## Design Variation Colors

### Data-Driven Variation

Use high contrast: Primary background, accent for highlight numbers, white text.

### Editorial Variation

Use sophisticated palette: Navy primary, muted secondary colors, serif typography.

### Technical Variation

Use dark palette with bright accent: Monospace fonts, geometric shapes.

### Story-Driven Variation

Use emotional palette: Warmer colors, serif display fonts, narrative hierarchy.

---

## WCAG AA Compliance

All text must meet WCAG AA color contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text.

Test your colors: https://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/
